Arithmetic operators

  • +- Addition
  • - - Subtraction
  • * - Multiplication
  • / - Division
  • % - Modulo Operation (Remainder after division
  • ++ - Increment
  • -- - Decrement
  • += - Addition Assignment
  • -= - Subtraction Assignment
  • *= - Multiplication Assignment
  • /= - Division Assignment
  • %= - Modulus Assignment
