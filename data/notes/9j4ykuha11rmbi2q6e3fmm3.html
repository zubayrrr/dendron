HTTP

  • Hyper Text Transfer Protocol, operates on Port 80 (Private).
  • It is the foundation for data communication for the WWW (Private).
  • Unsecured web browsing, as opposed to HTTPS.
  • Designed for distributed, collaborative and hypermedia presentation across many devices.
