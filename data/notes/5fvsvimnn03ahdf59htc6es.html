kill

  • Areas: Linux

kill is used to terminate a process.

It sends a signalto a process or a group of processes causing them to act according to the signal.

When the signal is not specified its default is: 15 or SIGTERM (Private)

kill -l to list all available signals: [[devlog.SIGHUP],SIGKILL (Private) etc

Besides kill, killall and pkill can also be used to send signals to processes.
