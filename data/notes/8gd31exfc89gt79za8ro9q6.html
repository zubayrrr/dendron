Enochian

  • Areas: Linguistics

Enochian is an occult constructed language — said by its originators to have been received from angels — recorded in the private journals of John Dee and his colleague Edward Kelley in late 16th-century England. Kelley was a scryer (Private) who worked with Dee in his magical investigations.
