SMTP

  • Simple Mail Transfer Protocol
  • Operates on Port 25
  • Internet standard for sending electronic mail
  • It was developed with the RFC (Private) 821, orginally defined in 1982
  • The current version was developed in 2008, defined in RFC (Private) 5321
