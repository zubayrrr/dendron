CSMA/CD

  • Areas: Networking
  • Ethernet devices transmit based on a principle called Carrier Sense Multiple Access/Collision Detect or CSMA/CD
  • Carrier Sense
    • Listen to the wire, verify it is not busy
  • Multiple Access
    • All devices have access at any time
  • Collision Detect
    • If two deviecs transmit at the same time, a collision occurs
    • Back off, wait a random time and try again
