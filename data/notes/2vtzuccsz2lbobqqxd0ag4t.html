Save tweets & threads permanently

Even if the original author deletes them, you'll still have your own copy saved in your dashboard forever.

Automatic export to note-taking apps

Just connect to Obsidian, Notion or Evernote (or all three) once and let the syncing happen automatically every 10 minutes behind-the-scenes

Daily & weekly highlights

Never forget what you saved with a dedicated highlights section to resurface what you saved today & this week (newsletter feature coming soon!)

Public unrolled thread URLs

Anytime you save a tweet/thread, you get a public URL with the unrolled contents to share with anyone (as if the tweet/thread was a blog post)

Save conversations between >2 people

If you see an engaging conversation between >2 people, you can save the conversation just like you would a tweet or thread!

Auto-save tweets you like

Just enable the setting and make liking a tweet automatically save the tweet to your dashboard, Notion, Evernote or Obsidian! (premium only)
