pkill

  • Areas: Linux

pkill is used to send signal to the processes of a running program based on given criteria. The processes can be specified by their full or partial names(unlike killall), a user running the process, or other attributes.

pkill is basically a wrapper around the pgrep program that only prints a list of matching processes.

Example

  • pkill processName or pkill partialProcessNa
