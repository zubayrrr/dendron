I have never let my schooling interfere with my education. – Mark Twain

Grant Allen wrote “No schooling was allowed to interfere with my education” in a variety of permutations. QI believes he probably originated the saying, and he clearly attempted to popularize it. However, Allen was a controversial figure and the maxim was reassigned to Mark Twain by 1907. — via Never Let Schooling Interfere With Your Education – Quote Investigator
