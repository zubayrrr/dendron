daemon

  • Areas: Linux

Daemons are the processes which run in the background and are not interactive and have no controlling terminal.

In Linux, the names of daemons end in d, colloquially daemons are known to be server or services, well known daemons are: sshd, devlog.httpd (Private), devlog.named (Private), devlog.mysqld (Private).
