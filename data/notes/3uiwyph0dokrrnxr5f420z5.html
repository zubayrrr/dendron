Patch Panels - Copper

  • Patch Panels are devices with jacks to connect wiring from the jack to a network Switch in a flexible manner.
  • On the back of these patch panels there are Punch Down Blocks (Private), looks like a 110 Block.
  • On the front of the patch panel looks like a network jack, RJ-45 jacks.
  • Patch panels are cheap and should be used as an intermediary between a plug and Switch to avoid damage to the Switch.
